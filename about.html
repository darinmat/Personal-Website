Travel and Cultural Exploration

Traveling has always been a passion of mine. Recently, I embarked on a month-long research trip to Japan, where I had the opportunity to delve into the impacts of the triple disasters of 3/11. This experience was both academically enriching and profoundly moving, as I explored the resilience and recovery of the affected communities. Following Japan, I extended my travels with a weeklong trip to Korea, immersing myself in the vibrant culture and history of the country. Each journey expands my worldview and deepens my appreciation for the diverse cultures that make up our world.

Technology in Sports

My fascination with the intersection of technology and sports has been a driving force in my academic and professional journey. I had the incredible opportunity to intern with the NBA, where I witnessed firsthand how data analytics and technology are revolutionizing the game. This experience was complemented by a school project where I developed ELO scores for NHL teams, showcasing my analytical skills and passion for sports statistics. Attending the MIT Sloan Sports Analytics Conference further fueled my interest, as I engaged with industry experts and explored the future of sports technology.
